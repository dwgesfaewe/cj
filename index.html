ABOUT US
CJ VENTURES is an international investment institution under the CJ Group, specializing in emerging digital technology industries. Leveraging its profound industry expertise and global resource network, it actively seeks out and invests in companies with innovative technologies and business models, supporting their rapid growth while also exploring new business areas and growth opportunities for the CJ Group.

This helps them achieve technological breakthroughs and market expansion, collectively building a digital technology ecosystem.
